Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 2026

1. Data We Collect

When you create an account, we collect your name and email address. As you use Spendly, we store the expense records you create — including amounts, categories, dates, and any descriptions you provide.

We do not collect payment information, precise location data, or any data beyond what is necessary to operate the service.

2. How We Use Your Data

Your data is used solely to provide the Spendly service — displaying your expenses, generating summaries, and enabling you to manage your financial records.

We do not use your data for advertising, profiling, or any purpose unrelated to the core functionality of the app.

3. Data Storage

Your data is stored on secured servers. We take reasonable technical and organisational measures to protect your information against unauthorised access, loss, or disclosure.

You may request deletion of your account and all associated data at any time by contacting us. Deletion requests are processed within 30 days.

4. Third Party Services

Spendly uses Google Fonts to load typography. This results in a request to Google's servers when you visit the site. No personal or financial data is shared with Google as part of this process.

We do not integrate with any third-party analytics, advertising, or data-sharing platforms.

5. Contact Us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy or wish to request access to or deletion of your data, please reach out to us at privacy@spendly.app.

We will respond to all privacy-related enquiries within 14 business days.
